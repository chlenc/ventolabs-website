## Notes
- All images should feel premium, professional, and tech-forward
- Color palette: dark backgrounds work best, accent with forest green (#234c12) or warm gold
- Avoid stock photo clichés (handshakes, pointing at whiteboards, generic laptops)
- Transparent PNG for the robot/mascot images, JPG for everything else
